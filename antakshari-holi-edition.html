Celebrate Holi with songs and music. Teams compete in this special Holi-themed Antakshari competition.

Event Rules & Guidelines

Team Structure

  • Total teams: 5
  • Members per team: 4

Round 1: Letter-Based Song Challenge

  • Songs must begin with the last letter of the previous song.
  • Time limit: 20 seconds to begin singing after the letter is announced.
  • No repetition of songs is allowed.
  • Languages permitted: Hindi, English, Punjabi, Haryanvi.

Round 2: Word-Based Song Challenge (Holi Special)

  • Buzzer round.
  • Song must contain the given word.
  • Team must say ‚ÄúHappy Holi‚Äù before singing.
  • The word must appear in the first lines of the song.
  • Only the exact word will be accepted.

Judging Criteria

  • Accuracy and rule compliance
  • Promptness of response
  • Team coordination
  • Confidence and stage presence
